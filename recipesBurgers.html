LASAGNE FAJITAS BURGERS
Ingredients:
Burgers:
  • 400 g black beans - rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 3/4 onion - finely chopped
  • 1/3 cup bead crumbs
  • 1/3 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1 cup walnuts
  • 1 tbsp somked paprika
  • 1 tbsp chilli powder
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • salt & pepper to taste
Sauce:
  • 1 cup vegan mayonnaise
  • 1.5 tsp mustard
  • 1 pickle - finely chopped
  • 1/4 onion - finely chopped
  • 1.5 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp vinegar
  • Salt to taste
To serve:
  • Burger buns or vegan brioche buns
  • Fresh tomatoes - sliced
  • Red onion - sliced into rings
  • Pickles - sliced
  • Lettuce, wild rocket or other leaves
  • French fries or chippy chips
Method:
  • Fry onion on a small dash of oil. When golden put it in a blender
  • Put walnuts, smoked paprika, chilli powder, garlic powder, salt and black pepper into your blender and blend together until smooth
  • Add fried onion, bread crumbs and BBQ sauce to the mixture and mix well
  • Mash black beans with a masher
  • Add mashed beans and cooked quinoa to the mix
  • Form burgers with your hand and shallow fry them for 5 minutes on each side
Sauce:
  • Mix all the ingredients in a bowl
  • If needed add a pinch of salt
Layering
  • Put one leaf of lettuce on a bun and cover it with the burger
  • On top of the burger put two slices of a tomato, a few slices of a pickle and some onion
  • Spread the sauce on the other slice of the bun and put it on top of the burger and its toppings
  • Serve with french fries or chippy chips

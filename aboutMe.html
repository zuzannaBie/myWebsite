Who am I?

My name is Zuzanna but everybody calls me Zazu. I have a Master's degree in Philosophy and a Bachelor's degree in French Philology. Most of my professional life I worked with marketing and PR angencies but my real passion has always been cooking. That is why I decided to create this blog to share my favourite recipes with all the vegan food lovers around the world!

My vegan journey

I was a vegetarian for over 10 years when one day I realised it just wasn't enough for me anymore. I switched to veganism 2 years ago and I could never go back.

Other interests

I obviously love to cook but having fun in the kitchen is not all that I am into. I also train aerial sports, play ukulele and recently I discovered that coding is so fascinating that I hope to make it my job soon :)
